General guideline

  • This is a weekly meeting. Each meeting is scheduled for approximately 75-minutes, with one speaker. The rough layout is a 45-min talk followed by a 30-min discussion, but timing can be (a bit) flexible.
  • We encourage participants, in particular students, to contact the organizer(s) to suggest specific speakers and/or topics they want to hear about.
    If you find someone you personally know from the "Guest Speaker Wishlist", please consider invite them over for a guest seminar.
  • This series aims to make current research topics more accessible to students and to celebrate research diversity. We therefore encourage speakers to tailor their talks specifically for students at the undergraduate and early graduate levels, as well as non-experts.
